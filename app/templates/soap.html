Welcome to Goat Mountain View Soap. I make small batches of high-quality soap using simple ingredients: goat milk, tallow, coconut oil, and olive oil. To create variety, I mix things up with essential oils, clays, botanicals, phthalate-free fragrances, and natural colorants (mineral oxides and mica). What makes Edit Soap special is the use of 9% butterfat milk from our Nigerian Dwarf dairy goats. Goat milk contains many compounds that are so good for your skin, such as alpha hydroxyl acids, essential fatty acids, triglycerides, vitamins A, B, C, D, and E, antioxidants, and selenium.
