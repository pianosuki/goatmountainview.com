Welcome to Goat Mountain View Soap. I craft small batches of high-quality soap using simple, wholesome ingredients: goat milk, tallow, coconut oil, and olive oil. To create variety, I enhance my formulations with essential oils, clays, botanicals, phthalate-free fragrances, and natural colorants such as mineral oxides and mica.

What makes GMV Soap special is the use of 9% butterfat milk from our Nigerian Dwarf dairy goats. Goat milk contains many beneficial compounds for your skin, including alpha hydroxyl acids, essential fatty acids, triglycerides, vitamins A, B, C, D, and E, antioxidants, and selenium.

Goat Mountain View Soap can be found at Nordiska gift shop on Front Street in Poulsbo.
Custom blends for Nordiska include the following:

Havet (Swedish for ocean; sea salt) • Blommig (floral; white magnolia) • Västkusten (West Coast; coastal rain scent) • Fika (coffee break; oatmeal, milk & honey) • Birka (birchwood; birchwood oud scent)
